Instant Pot Bourbon Pulled Pork

This pulled pork is so easy, it takes the work out of cooking

Tired of waiting hours and hours by the slow cooker for the perfect pulled pork sandwiches? Well this recipe will save you so much time, as it uses the magical Instant Pot, and it does so without sacrificing flavor or tenderness.

With just about an hour of total cook time, you'll have your hungry dinner guests served this delicious entree before they can even ask 'when's supper ready?'

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork tenderloin, cut into 2" chunks
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp fresh ground pepper
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp cayenne
  • 1 tbsp chile powder
  • 2 cups barbecue sauce of your choice
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups chicken or beef broth
  • splash of tobasco sauce
  • half cup of your choice of bourbon

Directions

  1. Using a medium-sized bowl, mix dry seasonings until homogeneous
  2. Coat 2" chunks of pork tenderloin entirely in seasoning mixture
  3. Set Instant Pot to saute setting and heat oil. Then using tongs, fry all sides of the pork until exterior is brown

    Don't worry about cooking through, this is just to sear the meat. It will cook thoroughly in the next steps

  4. Remove pork from Instant Pot and pour in 1 cup of broth. Using a wooden spoon, deglaze the pot
  5. Set the Instant Pot to pressure cook setting. Stir together the remaining broth, 1 cup of barbecue sauce, the tobasco sauce, bourbon, and the apple cider vinegar, and add mixture to the pot
  6. Return the seared pork to the pot with the mixture and seal. Set cook time for 40 minutes
  7. After 40 minutes, let the pot release pressure naturally for 10 minutes, then hit the quick release
  8. Drain liquid from the pot, then stire in the remaining 1 cup of barbecue sauce
  9. Using a fork, pull the pork so it's stringy. The should should be tender enough that it shouldn't take much effort at all
  10. Use the pulled pork for sandwiches, tacos, topping for nachos, or whatever else your creative little brain can think of. Enjoy!

Easy Pulled Pork Nachos

These nachos, baked in the oven, are an ideal appetizer to any meal

This recipe should feed up to 6 dinner guests, or 1-2 hungry fat guys.

Use Kory's Instant Pot Bourbon Pulled Pork as the perfect topping!

Ingredients

  • Half a bag of your favorite tortilla chips
  • 1 jar of queso
  • 1-2 pounds of your favorite meat. Kory recommends his renowned, world-famous Instant Pot Bourbon Pulled Pork.
  • 1 bag of shredded mexican cheese
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • optionalSliced jalepeno peppers to taste
  • optionalSour cream to taste
  • optionalGuacamole to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit
  2. Using a large cast-iron skillet, lay a bed of tortilla chips, followed by the meat of your choice, then the shredded mexican cheese, queso, onions, and finally the tomatoes.
  3. Repeat that step for 2 more layers, then top with jalepenos, if desired
  4. Bake for approximately 10 minutes
  5. Top with sour cream and guacamole, if desired
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
